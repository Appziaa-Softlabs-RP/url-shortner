## Metrics
Track the performance of your URLs with ease:
1. Go to the dashboard and locate your list of URLs.
2. Click on a specific URL to view detailed analytics, including:
   - Total clicks
   - Referrer sources
   - Geographic data
3. Alternatively, open the short URL to access its analytics.
